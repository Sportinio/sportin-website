Structure

How it works.

01
Register
Sign up your brand. Build your squad, minimum 10 players, no upper limit. Employees, clients, ambassadors, communities, partners. Anyone who moves.
02
Match
Every Saturday, a 24-hour match. The team that covers the most distance takes 3 points. Weekly or biweekly rhythm depending on the league calendar.
03
Climb
National league table. Every point matters. Every matchday reshuffles the standings. Five months of momentum, tension, and brand visibility.
04
Win
The season ends with a champion. One brand lifts the National League Cup. Top finishers qualify for the International League of Brands.
